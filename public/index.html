    We have various methods of payment. The payment gateways frequently used are Paypal, Payoneer, and Direct bank payment.
    What is the currency used for custom quotes?
    All prices for all our services are rated in USD.
    Who do we render our services to?
    Our services span throughout every industry, ranging from authors, businesses, academics, thought leaders, researchers, corporate and private organizations.
