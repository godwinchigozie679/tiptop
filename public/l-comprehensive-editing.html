Comprehensive Editing Services

Our standard comprehensive editing services include:

  1. Regular Book Editing Services – correcting grammar, spelling, punctuation, verb tenses, transitions, word phrasing, and clarity of writing
  2. Content Editing Services – reviewing the plot and character elements for consistency in fiction and nonfiction works
  3. Rewriting of sentences, when necessary
  4. Professional comments and feedback regarding overall writing style, flow of presentation and initial impressions by the editor
  5. Rewriting, when necessary
  6. Research Editing Services – formatting of citations, references and footnotes according to your requested style guide / manual of style (APA, MLA, Chicago, etc.)
  7. Critique – of the entire manuscript.
